Home aided
NAIROBI: Four bands on Thursday night helped to raise more than Sh45,000 for charity during a musical show at Legacy Africa Restaurant. The money was presented to the founder of Nyumbani Home, Father Angelo D'Agostino. The home cares for 25 children born with HIV, the virus that causes Aids. Bilega Musica, Bogos Musica, Malembe Stars and Bora Bora Sounds Band took part.
