{"text": "Tuesday March 27 1990\n\nAids: WHO pleads with US\nBy NATION Reporter\u2028\nThe World Health Organization\n\n(WHO) has asked the United States to drop all travel restrictions against HIV-infected\n\nPersons. The WHO director-general, Dr.Hiroshi Nakajima, said in a statement \u201cOur priority is and will remain the full protection of human rights of HIV-infected people and people with Aids.\u201d\nHe said the WHO\u2019s decision was in keeping with the 1988 World Health Assembly Resolution. He urged all member states to respect this resolution against the discrimination of HIV and Aids victims."}
