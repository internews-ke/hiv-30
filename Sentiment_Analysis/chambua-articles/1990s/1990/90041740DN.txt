{"text": "April 27, 1990\nAids: WHO pleads with US\nBy NATION Reporter\n\nThe World Health Organisation (WHO) has asked the United States to drop all travel restrictions against HIV-infected persons.\n\nThe WHO director-general, Dr Hiroshi Nakajima, said in a statement: \"Our priority is and\n..will remain the full protection of human rights of' HIV-infected people and people with Aids.\"\nHe said the WHO's decision was in keeping with the 1988 World Health Assembly Resolution.\nHe urged all member states to respect this resolution against the discrimination of HIV and\nAids victims"}
