{"text": "Aids orphans set to get free education\nBy NATION Correspondent \nThe Government plans to provide free education to children orphaned by HIV/Aids.\nThis was disclosed yesterday by the national coordinator for information, education and communication. Mr Meshack Ndolo, during a regional HIV/Aids workshop at a Machakos hotel.\nOutlining the 'Ministry of Health's policy on HIV/Aids, Mr Ndolo said the policy was intended to ensure' that disadvantaged children continue with education.\nHe said, the move was part of the Government's efforts to achieve, universal primary education.\nMr Ndolo said the Ministry, of Education and the Children's Department, as well as permanent secretaries drawn from various ministries will, if the policy succeeds, form committees to come up with modalities on how to make the project a reality."}
