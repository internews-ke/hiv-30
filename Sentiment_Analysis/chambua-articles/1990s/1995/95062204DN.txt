{"text": "Plea for Aids victims\nBy NATION Correspondent\nAids victims should be retained in employment as long as they have the strength to work, a Nairobi legal officer has said.\nMrs Jackline Mugo said employers demoralised employees by stopping them from working after they contracted the virus.\nShe was addressing an Aids awareness forum organised by the Red Cross Society of Kenya at the Kenya National Theatre last night.\nA participant urged insurance firms not to discriminate against HIV positive people."}
