{"text": "Plea on Aids\nNAIROBI: Health Minister Joshua Angatia yesterday urged church leaders to promote the use of condoms as a way of controlling the spread of Aids. Mr Angatia, addressing the first Ecumenical Conference on HIV/Aids; the religious perspective of its social, diagnostic and caring aspects, said people should be advised to abstain and stick to one sex partner but if the two fail, they should use condoms."}
