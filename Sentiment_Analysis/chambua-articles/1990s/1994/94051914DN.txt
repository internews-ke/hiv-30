{"text": "May 19 94\nLunch on Aids\nNAIROBI: The Kenya Aids Society has organised a luncheon for\nlawyers today at the Hilton Hotel.\nThere will be a discussion on\nthe Role of Law and the Lawyer\nin protecting the civil rights of\npeople with Aids. This is one of\nthe events to mark the 11th International Aids Candlelight Memorial\nMobilisation Day whose\ntheme is \"Protecting the rights of\npeople living with Aids and HIV\"."}
