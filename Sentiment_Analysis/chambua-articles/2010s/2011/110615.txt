{"text": "HIV schemes to include drug users \nIn Kenya, a new HIV/AIDS program is aiming to reduce the number of infections amongst prostitutes and drug users. In the past, these groups have been stigmatized and left out of prevention and treatment initiatives, despite the fact that the make up a disproportionate percentage of Kenya\u2019s HIV-positive population. The new scheme, financed by the Global Fund, will provide free needle replacements in hospitals, in order to try and limit the number of infections that are transmitted through drug use. "}
