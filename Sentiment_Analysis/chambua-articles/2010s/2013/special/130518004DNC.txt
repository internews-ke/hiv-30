{"text": "KISUMU\nOfficials gather in town for regional Aids talks\nA regional conference on the impact of HIV and Aids in the Lake Victoria Basin starts tomorrow. The second East African Community Regional Cross-border Transport Corridor HIV and Aids meeting brings together participants from five member states. Government officials and representatives of non-governmental and donor organizations are also expected to the conference that runs to Friday.
