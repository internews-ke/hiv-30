Monday May 18, 2009 DAILY NATION

KISUMU

Officials gather in town
for regional Aids talks

A regional conference on the impact
of HIV and Aids in the Lake
Victoria basin starts tomorrow. The
second East African Community Regional
Cross-border Transport Corridor
HIV and Aids meeting brings
together participants from the fi ve
member states. Government offi cials
and representatives of non-governmental
and donor organisations are
also expected at the conference that
runs to Friday. Activities include
visits to Usenge beach in Bondo district
and Malaba town on the Kenya-
Uganda border.
