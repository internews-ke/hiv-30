Wednesday October 14, 2009

NAIROBI
Sh7m boost for Aids
and diabetes study
A local research institute has
been awarded a Sh7.5 million
grant to do research on the
link between type 2 diabetes
and HIV resistance. The Bill
and Melinda Gates Foundation
programme offi cer, Dr
Andrew Serazin, yesterday
said the one-year funding to
Kenya Medical Research Institute
scientists, led by Prof
Maritim Songo, will enable
the researchers to study a
hypothesis of a link between
diabetes type 2 and HIV resistance.
