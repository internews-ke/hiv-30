        Training Centre, the PSC will work to
        promote social justice and equity so as to
        ensure training activities are grounded in
        experience and address contemporary
        concerns in poverty and policy.
            Since its establishment in 1989, over
         100 organisations, among them a large
        number of grass roots institutions have
        utilized resources at the center to hone
        their activities geared at promoting
        people-centred development.
            These are diverse, including
        governance, gender equity, sustainable
        livelihoods, the Aids pandemic and
        advocacy.
