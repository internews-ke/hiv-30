HIv infections' alert

The Koibatek District Medical officer
of Health, Dr Samuel Amwayi, has
urgedtesidents to undergo voluntary
HIV tests. Dr Amwayi said that, out of
the 53.8 cases tested in the district
recently, 69 werepositive. Speaking during
the launch of the Aids Committee at
Eldama Ravine, he said it was important
fot one to know one's HIV status.
