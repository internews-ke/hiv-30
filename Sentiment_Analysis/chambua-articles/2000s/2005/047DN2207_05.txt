ASSISTANTS 2 POSITIONS
The Walter Reed Project Medical Research
Institute[WRP/KEMRI] project is inviting applications
for an Accounts Assistants Positions for HIV?AIDS
program based in Kericho.
Duties:
The Accounts assistants will assist the program
Accountants in mantaining the relevant books of
accounts, preparations of financial reports and to
oversee and monitor accounting transactions and
proper documentation from the partner institutions
funded by the program.
Qualifications:
Applicants must have at least CPA II, with not less
than 2years experience in similar position. Knowledge
of computerized accounting will be an added
advantage. The successful candidates will be appointed
on a six month contract renewable by mutual consent.
Send your application letter, curriculum vitae and
copies of certificates to:-
The Administrator,
The Walter Reed Project/KEMRI Clinical Research
Centre,
P.O Box 1357, Hospital Road, Kericho, Kenya
Deadline for submission: August 10th 2005
