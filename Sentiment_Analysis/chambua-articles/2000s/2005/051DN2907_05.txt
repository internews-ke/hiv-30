Daily Nation/ Friday, July 29, 2005
EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITY
The United States Embassy – Centers for Disease Control
and Prevention (CDC) Nairobi has a vacancy for a
Technical Advisor (HIV Programs). The incumbent will
be responsible for working directly with South Sudan
program coordinator in planning and implementation of CDC
supported HIV-related program in Sudan, including
surveillance, PMTCT, counseling and testing, care and
treatment and HIV control efforts in the uniformed services
of South Sudan.
Requirement: A Master’s Degree in public health, nursing or
related health or social welfare training. Five years of work
experience of which at least 2 years in Public Health or
HIV/AIDS programs. Must have experience with budgets
and word processing and spreadsheets computer programs.
Beginning annual salary inclusive of allowances will be
Kshs. 1,748,846.00.
Those fulfilling the requirements of the position should
submit their application together with a detailed CV and all
relevant attachments via mail before August 9, 2005 to the
following address:
Human Resources Office
Public Health Professional Positions
P. O. Box 606
Village Market
00621 Nairobi, Kenya
