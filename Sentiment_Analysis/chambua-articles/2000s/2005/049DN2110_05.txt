Daily Nation/ Friday, October 21, 2005
KEMRI CGMRC WELLCOME TRUST RESEARCH PROGRAMME, KILIFI
Social scientist, KEMRI-Kilifi
KEMRI in collaboration with Kilifi District Hospital (KDH) is strengthening its
HIV care and prevention services.
The Centre is seeking an enthusiastic and creative person to promote VCT and
couple VCT. The successful candidate will be required to liase between HIV
research project and the Kilifi community.
The applicant should be able to:
• Initiate and manage an active HIV education and prevention programme
• Promote VCT, and CVCT to the communities and reduce HIV stigma
• Train and supervise various teams of community advocates who will assist
in further promotion of couple VCT.
• Liase with CBO and NGO groups to promote services at the Community HIV
Centre and Family Health Clinic at the KDH
• Initiate and maintain a resource centre for HIV/ AIDS and related research
materials
The successful candidate should have the following skills, experiences and
qualifications:
• Effective communication skills at grass root and higher levels
• Good organisation and management skills
• Bachelor’s degree in Social Science or equivalent.
• Proficiency with word processing, spreadsheets and databases
• Should be fluent in Kiswahili and Giryama language.
Applications should preferably be by e-mail and include a full CV, the names
and contacts of two referees and a covering letter with details of current
employment. Please include a telephone contact number.
Closing date for application is 2nd November 2005. Only short-listed applicants
will be contacted for interview.
