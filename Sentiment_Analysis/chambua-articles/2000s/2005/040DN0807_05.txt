Daily Nation/ Friday, July 8, 2005
VACANCY
LABORATORY MANAGER
The University of Manitoba is seeking exceptional candidates for the
position of full-time Laboratory Manager for the University of
Nairobi /Manitoba research laboratory at World Health
Organization STD/ HIV Collaborative Centre based in Nairobi,
Kenya. The mission of this Project is to study sexually transmitted
infections including HIV / AIDS and the programs conducted are national
and international in scope and involve laboratory, epidemiologic and
clinical research; surveillance; and development, implementation and
evaluation of prevention programs. The laboratory is also committed to
training future technical and scientific staff.
RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:
• providing the scientific, programmatic and management leadership
required for running the research laboratory, which includes such
organizational units as bacteriology, serology, immunology and
molecular diagnostics. Assisting in the oversight of a laboratory
construction project, on-going management of those facilities and the
development of a business plan.
QUALIFICATIONS
The applicant must possess an, M.Sc., M.D. (or equivalent) or Ph.D., and
demonstrate:
(1) a high degree of laboratory management experience, including
knowledge of international standards for good laboratory practices
(GLP);
(2) the ability to direct and manage a research laboratory including
establishing strategic plans, policies and standard operating
procedures; developing, presenting and adhering to budgets;
evaluating program accomplishments; and building and maintaining a
well trained staff; and
(3) experience that indicates the ability to deal effectively with scientists
from the collaborating institutions. Salary and benefits will
commensurate with qualifications and experience.
Please send an up-to-date Resumé, and names and addresses of three
references, by email (preferably) or post by August 15, 2005, to Dr.
Larry Gelmon, World Health Organization STD/HIV
Collaborative Project, P.O.Box 19676-Nairobi, Kenya or email to
lgelmon@csrtkenya.org
