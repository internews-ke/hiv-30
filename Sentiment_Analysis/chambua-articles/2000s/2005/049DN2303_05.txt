ST. JOSEPH’S MISSION HOSPITAL MIGORI
CATHOLIC DIOCESE OF HOMA-BAY
VACANT POSITIONS
There are two vacancies at St. Joseph’s Mission Hospital, Migori
under President Bush Emergency Program for Aids Relief in
Africa (PEPFAR) on Anti Retroviral Therapy Project (ART) –
Coordinated by Catholic Relief Services (CRS) for five years
renewable yearly.
A. MEDICAL OFFICER OF HEALTH
With the following qualifications and experience:-
• Registered with the Kenya Medical Practitioner and Dentist
Board
• At least one year Post internship
• Had some training on HIV and Anti Retroviral Therapy
• Be a committed Christian with good interpersonal
relationship with patients and colleagues.
B. ASSISTANT ACCOUNTANT
Qualifications: At least CPA, Section II
• Hands-on experience in working with computerized
accounting System, excel spreadsheets and word.
• At least 1 year experience in a busy finance office in a
similar position.
• NGO experience is an added advantage.
Applications including a cover letter and detailed CV should be
submitted to the undersigned not later than 6th April, 2005.
The salary will be negotiated but competitive with fringe benefits.
Three referees of which one must be a Priest or a Pastor.
