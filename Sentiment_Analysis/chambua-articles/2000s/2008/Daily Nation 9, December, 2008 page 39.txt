Daily Nation 9, December, 2008 page 39

CLERGY TO GO FOR HIV TEST

BY SILAS NTHIGA
Nine Anglican Church of Kenya bishops from Mt. Kenya region have 
challenged the clergy to lead in the war against HIV and AIDS by getting tested.

And to demonstrate their resolve to fight the scourge, the bishops have directed the clergy in their dioceses to undergoe the test and establish their health status within the next two weeks.
In a joint statement issued after a five day conference at St. marks Teachers college in Embu, he Bishos asked the governemnt to step up the war against AIDS by changing its strategies.
