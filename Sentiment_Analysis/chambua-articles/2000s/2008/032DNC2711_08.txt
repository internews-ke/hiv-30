Thursday November 27, 2008
NAKURU
Beauty contest seeks
to fight Aids stigma
A beauty contest that seeks to eradicate
stigma and discrimination linked
to HIV and Aids will be held in Nakuru
on Saturday. According to the organiser
Nana Warinda, youth groups
were taking part in putting together
the Ms Red Ribbon contest that will
take place at Waterbuck Hotel. The
World Aids Day will be marked on
Monday at the Nyayo Garden in the
town and retired president Moi is expected
to officiate.
