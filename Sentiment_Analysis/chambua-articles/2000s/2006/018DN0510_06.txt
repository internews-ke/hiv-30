Daily Nation/ Thursday, October 5, 2006

Charity’s project
to help Aids fight
The United States Agency for
International Development has
launched the second phase of a
five-year project in Nyanza province
to help the fight against
Aids. The charity is to run the
Aphia Nyanza II project at a cost
of Sh1.5 billion, said the project
director, Dr Job Obwaka. He
added that it would help the ministry
of Health and other parties
to improve reproductive health.
