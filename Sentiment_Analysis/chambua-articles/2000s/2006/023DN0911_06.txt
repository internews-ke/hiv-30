Daily Nation / Thursday, November 9, 2006
Vacancy: Project Coordinator – Nyanza region
KickStart and FACES (Family AIDS Care and Education Services)
partnership are seeking to recruit a Project Coordinator whose
primary objective will be to manage the project effectively for both
FACES and KICKSTART coalition under grant funding. Main
responsibilities include program design; working with HIV affected
/ infected farmers with entrepreneurial skills currently under the
care of FACES; develop annual work plans; write concept papers;
mitigate fragmentation of the food security program and health
program by adding value and ensure expansion of the pilot
projects to other areas with a view of improving wealth for health;
Alliance building among others.
Skills required:-
a) Demonstrated written and oral communication skills.
b) Practical understanding of HIV/AIDS health related issues,
community development process and socio-economic
analysis.
c) Must have strong knowledge of agriculture through enhanced
crop production for food security and income generation
d) Strong project management skills and Micro finance
knowledge (working experience required)
e) Proven ability to develop work plans, budgets and proposals
f) Experience in working with rural communities on
development, health and food security issues in liaison with
donors.
g) Must be a holder of a valid motorbike license
Academic qualifications:
a} Minimum degree in Social sciences (Agriculture, Home
science with a bias in nutrition, Sociology, Food technology,
Community Development)
b} Post graduate diploma or degree in Project Management
To apply for this opportunity, please submit detailed re’sume / CV
(one file) with cover letter to Recruitment@Kickstart.org within 14
days from the date of posting. Only successful candidates will be
contacted. Visit our websites: www.kickstart.org ,
www.faces-kenya.org
