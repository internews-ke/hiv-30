Daily Nation/ Wednesday, April 5, 2006

MINISTRY OF HEALTH/NASCOP
In conjunction with the Ministry of Health, the Capacity Project of IntraHealth International Inc.,
seeks to fill the position of Clinical Care and Public Health Advisor at National AIDS and STD
Control Program (NASCOP). This is a fulltime position funded by USAID and based at the NASCOP
Headquarters in Nairobi. The primary responsibility of the position is to assist in the development of
systems to support the roll-out of anti-retroviral therapy to AIDS patients throughout Kenya.
Essential Functions:
• Develop and strengthen coordination across the different HIV care and ART programs;
• Support the development and the use of management information systems in patient and drug
flow and link the two together to inform the ART roll-out process and advise the Director and
Coordinator accordingly;
• Develop tools for ART program monitoring and evaluation (process and outcome) in line with
national requirements and international norms and standards;
• Support the development of tools to strengthen standards setting and quality assurance mechanisms
in different levels and facets of the program and be responsive to technological developments
in these fields to build capacity;
• Enhance skills at the provincial and district levels
Education/Experience Required:
• Postgraduate qualification – MD/MMED with MPH or other relevant health related postgraduate
qualification.
• Experience with HIV/AIDS care and treatment issues
• Experience in program development and management
• Demonstrated understanding of management information systems, and technical tools for monitoring
and evaluation within the public health sector in Africa or a developing country
• Strong computer skills.
• Epidemiological background would be useful.
