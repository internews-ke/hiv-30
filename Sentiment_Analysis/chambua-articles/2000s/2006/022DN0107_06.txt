Saturday Nation / July 1, 2006

HIV/AIDS Programme
St. John runs an HIV/AIDS programme that mainly deals with Home Based
Care, Peer Education and Voluntary Counselling and Testing Centres.
The St. John HIV/AIDS program has benefited many people so far. 1,134
community Health Workers have been trained in various parts of Kenya and are
providing services to infected and affected families.
The St. John Voluntary Counseling and Testing Centre (VCT) is at the
Headquarters at St. John House near Parliament buildings. The Centre is open
from 8.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. Monday to Friday. St. John also organizes Mobile
VCT Clinics for organized groups and communities.
