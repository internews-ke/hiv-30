Daily Nation/ Friday, September 8, 2006
Zimbabwe Aids
infections drop

HARARE, Thursday
The number of of people infected with
the virus that causes Aids is down in
Zimbabwe due to increased awareness.
However, the country still has one of
the highest HIV rates in the world, state
media reported today.
Health Minister David Parirenyatwa
was quoted by the Herald newspaper as
saying the latest demographic health
survey showed that the country’s HIV
prevalence rate had declined to 18.1 per
cent from 20.1 per cent from 2004.
The survey showed a rise in condom
use, a delay by young girls in starting sex
and an Aids awareness rate of up to 99.2
per cent. Aids kills about 3,000 Zimbabweans
every week. (Reuters)
