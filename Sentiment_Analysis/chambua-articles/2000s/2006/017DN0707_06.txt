Daily Nation/ Friday, July 7, 2006
The United States Embassy – Centers for Disease Control
(CDC) Kenya has a vacancy in Nairobi for a Technical
Advisor - Prevention of HIV Mother to
Child Transmission (PMCT). The incumbent of
the position will supervise all PMCT work for CDC GAP in
Kenya at selected delivery and study sites.
Requirements: Medical qualification, (MBChB or equivalent);
and Master’s degree in Public Health, Pediatrics or
Obstetrics and Gynecology. Five years of experience in
HIV/AIDS related field. Must have advanced knowledge of
program development, evaluation, and principles of
epidemiology, including operational research designs and
statistical methods applicable to epidemiological studies,
public health surveillance, programmatic interventions, and
program evaluation. Must have extensive knowledge
regarding HIV/AIDS in general and PMCT specifically and
experience in translating research findings in programmatic
guidance, and be able to clearly explain research issues and
their implications to non-epidemiologists.
Those fulfilling the requirements of the position should
submit their application together with a detailed CV and all
relevant attachments via mail before July 18, 2006 to the
following address:
Human Resources Office
Public Health Professional Positions
P. O. Box 606
Village Market
00621 Nairobi, Kenya
