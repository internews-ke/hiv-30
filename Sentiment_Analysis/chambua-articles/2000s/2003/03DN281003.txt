Daily Nation! Tuesday, October 28, 2003

NEW MEDIA AWARD FOR AIDS REPORTING

A Media award for journalists from
Eastern and Southern Africa writing on
Aids was launched in Nairobi yesterday.
It was launched by Unesco and
UNAIDS. Mr Alonso Aznar, the
- Unesco's communications advisor for
Eastern Africa, said the three winners
would get 1,500 dollars each. Articles
published or broadcast between
December 1, 2002, and November 15,
2003, can be submitted to Media and
HIV Aids Kelin Network, P.O. Box
1038, Village Market, Nairobi.
