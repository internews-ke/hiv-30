    However, the establishment of a lottery is not
    possible without legislation since the law that
    created the Kenya Charity Sweepstake, the
    only national lottery in Kenya, prohibits the
    establishment of any other such lottery.
    The document, when finally passed by parlia-
    ment where it is due for discussion in a
    month's time, is intended to be the reference   .
    and guideline document in sports, to establish
    which the management and development of
•   the national plan and guiding principles on
    sports will be based.
    The government also proposes to encourage
    corporate sports sponsorship by giving tax
    rebates to companies which support sports in
    cash and kind. There is also a proposal to
    waive value added tax on material for con-
    struction and maintenance of sports facilities.
    The document recognises the role sports can
    play in the eradication of poverty and pro-
    poses to motivate sports by among other
    things, honouring living and dead sportsmen
    and women through a Hall of Fame where
    such Kenyans can be immortalised, conferring
    state awards and medals to excelling sports
    people and the establishment of an insurance
    savings scheme to cater for the needs of
    retired sports personalities in old age.
