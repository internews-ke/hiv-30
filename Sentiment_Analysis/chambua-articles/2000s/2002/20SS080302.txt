                                                                                                                                       necks or clash of interests between

                                                                                                                                       fear of stigma
                                                                                                                                            With all these challenges on her
                                                                                                                                       path to a holistic maturity, the Kenyan
                                                                                                                                       girl is caught between the devil and
                                                                                                                                       the deep sea Our youth are left to
                                                                                                                                       grope in the dark as they cope with
                                                                                                                                       the challenges of adolescence.
                                                                                                                                            It is true that our children are now
                                                                                                                                      starting sex at a vel)' tenqer age. This
                                                                                                                                      can be blamed on social change and
                                                                                                                                      Western media influences, which are
                                                                                                                                      difficult to control. It is no longer real-
                                                                                                                                      istic to preach abstinence for youth.
                                                                                                                                           What we need to recognise is that
                                                                                                                                      healthy sexuality is a young person's
                                                                                                                                      right. With full information and ser-
                                                                                                                                      vices, an environment of metered per-

                                                                                                                                      choices about their reproductive
                                                                                                                                      health - whether to delay sex until
                                                                                                                                      marriage or to become sexually active
                                                                                                                                      prematurely and sink.
