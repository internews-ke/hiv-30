                                                                                                           SENIOR PROGRAMME OFFICER: DISASTER RISK REDUCTION: REF NO. EAZO/DM/PRO/01/2009
                                                                                                         The senior Programme Ofﬁcer will coordinate the implementation of a disaster reduction programme through a
                                                                                                         comprehensive series of activities implemented at a national and zone level, strengthen community resilience to
                                                                                                         disasters by investing in local level risk reduction, strengthening humanitarian response and advancing learning
                                                                                                         through innovative knowledge management. Within the context of existing and emerging disaster management
                                                                                                         agenda – including resolutions from the 28th Red Cross International Conference and the 2005 World Conference
                                                                                                         on Disaster Reduction, Kobe – the Senior Programme Ofﬁcer will, either directly or through Federation country
                                                                                                         delegations, where they exist, support national societies to:
                                                                                                          1. Establish small-scale local level risk reduction projects and preparedness for response measures that strengthen
                                                                                                             disaster resilience within the Framework for Community Resilience developed by the Federation in 2008;
                                                                                                          2. Signiﬁcantly strengthen local, national and regional disaster management capacities to ensure timely and
                                                                                                             effective humanitarian response; and
                                                                                                          3. Scale up knowledge management and lesson learning mechanisms/tools to both inﬂuence policy and
                                                                                                             practice as well as to strengthen engagement with a wider group of practitioners/policy makers.
                                                                                                         The key tasks for the position are:
                                                                                                          • Manage regional disaster reduction activities and provide technical and managerial support to activities at
                                                                                                            country level (managed by national societies) in full coordination with the Federation, National Societies the
                                                                                                            International Committee of the Red Cross and in close cooperation with the British Red Cross, DFID and other
                                                                                                            key stakeholders.
                                                                                                          • Establish support for national societies to establish, comprehensive programme plans – including programme
                                                                                                            baselines – in accordance with the Phase II programme proposal.
                                                                                                          • Manage all aspects of the DFID disaster reduction Phase II programme in accordance with detailed country
                                                                                                            and regional plans.
                                                                                                          • Support national society staff and volunteers through technical advice or training in all aspects of the
                                                                                                            programme.
                                                                                                          • Facilitate programme monitoring, including regular ﬁnancial and narrative reporting as well as innovative lesson
                                                                                                            learning, and evaluation based on programme plans.
                                                                                                          • Guide and support Federation and national societies to develop regional and national plans that can be
                                                                                                            readily consolidated into both a regional 3 year plan and more detailed annual action plans ensuring that all
                                                                                                            activities are closely linked and coordinated with wider national and regional disaster management plans.
                                                                                                         TARGET PROFILE
                                                                                                         A bachelor’s degree in disaster management or any other relevant ﬁeld; post graduate qualiﬁcations is desired.
