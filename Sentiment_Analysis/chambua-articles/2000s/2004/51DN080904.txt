AFC Leopards
founder member
Ben Amimo is
recovering at the Masaba Hospital
in Nairobi after he was involved in
an accident a few days ago. Nai·
robi soccer official Hussein Keya
said on Monday that Amimo suf-
fered leg injuries and was taken to
hospital by former KFF chairman
Maina Kariuki. He thanked Kariuki
for paying part of Amimo's hospi-
tal bill and appealed to soccer lov-
ers to contribute to his remaining
bill.
The Haben Trust Sports Against
Drugs and HIV/AIDS will hold its
second beach volleyball tourna-
ment at the Jadini Beach Hotel on
the South Coast on Saturday.
According to the organiser, Sophie
Schneid, the event is held to help
clean Diani beach of crime, prosti-
tution, drugs and HIV Aids. Sch-
neid added that the event is also
to make the people at the South
Coast engage more in sports.
