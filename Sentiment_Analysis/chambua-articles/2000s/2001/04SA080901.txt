Saturday Nation/ September 8, 2001

BRIEFS

State gives Aids groups Sh135,OOO

More than 850 people in Coast Province
have voluntarily been counselled
and tested for HIV. The Government
has established five centres for HIV
testing and counselling in Kilifi District
to combat the spread of Aids, the
Coast Provincial Aids co-ordinator,
Dr Esther Getambu, said. The centres
have been established at the Kilifi
District Hospital, Mariakani, Vipingo,
Bamba and Mtwapa Health Centres.
The centres will provide information
on access to anti-retroviral drugs and
nutritional needs. Dr Getambu was
speaking during the distribution of
more than Sh135,360 to Bahari, Ganze
and Kaloleni constituency aids control
committees from the National Aids
Control Council. The Kilifi District
Commissioner, Mr Oku Kaunya,
advised the three constituency aids
control committees to update their
records of Aids orphans and widows.
