                                                                                                                                   And this brings me to what’s become a talking point: abortion.
                                                                                                                                Being a human rights activist, I tread on this area with utmost
                                                                                                                                caution because I know that, like with any coin, there are always
                                                                                                                                two sides to any story. Many times I’ve never known what to
                                                                                                                                say when confronted by the debates of pro-life and pro-choice.
                                                                                                                                I’m not alone. In this country, it seems as if the battle lines are
                                                                                                                                drawn.
                                                                                                                                   Abortion is an emotional subject. It concerns the termination
                                                                                                                                of life. It’s a soul-searching and heartbreaking issue, which I
                                                                                                                                think shouldn’t be lumped together with health and family
                                                                                                                                planning. What may be less obvious, but will become clearer
                                                                                                                                overtime - I believe - is that abortion is a sin and a crime. There.
                                                                                                                                Now you know which side of the picket line I’m on. I’m not pro-
                                                                                                                                choice.
