                                                                                                                                                                                              • Fulfil promises
                                                                                                                                                                                             Alleviating poverty costs money.
                                                                                                                                                                                             Admirable goals and a strong will
                                                                                                                                                                                             do not suffice. At the Special Ses-
                                                                                                                                                                                             sion of the UN General Assem-
                                                                                                                                                                                             bly on HIV/Aids in June, mem- ~
                                                                                                                                                                                             ber countries agreed on the need
                                                                                                                                                                                             for a global fund to be set up to
                                                                                                                                                                                             intensify the fight against the
                                                                                                                                                                                             scourge. The annual capital
                                                                                                                                                                                             requirement of the fund wasesti-
                                                                                                                                                                                             mated at about $6 billion but
                                                                                                                                                                                             only about       $1 billion     was
                                                                                                                                                                                             pledged. In the same breath, the
                                                                                                                                                                                             commitment to increase aid to
                                                                                                                                                                                             0.7 per cent of BNI was reiterat-
                                                                                                                                                                                             ed, a pledge echoed as a mantra
                                                                                                                                                                                             by all countries, world confer-
                                                                                                                                                                                             ences and special sessions for
                                                                                                                                                                                             over 20 years. If this pledge was
                                                                                                                                                                                             honoured, world aid would rise
                                                                                                                                                                                             by $100 billion and the financing
                                                                                                                                                                                             of this fund, for example, would
                                                                                                                                                                                             be solved.
                                                                                                                                                                                                The rich countries       cannot
                                                                                                                                                                                             afford to allow the gap between
                                                                                                                                                                                             rhetoric and practice to become
                                                                                                                                                                                             an abyss. The divide already
                                                                                                                                                                                             exists; in Africa, aid per capita
                                                                                                                                                                                             has fallen to half the level it was
                                                                                                                                                                                             10 years ago.
                                                                                                                                                                                                Aid is needed, and it needs to
                                                                                                                                                                                             be increased. It can stimulate
                                                                                                                                                                                             processes and pave the way for
                                                                                                                                                                                             investment. It enables poor coun-
                                                                                                                                                                                             tries to participate in interna-
                                                                                                                                                                                             tional cooperation. But halving
                                                                                                                                                                                             world poverty is not just a devel-
                                                                                                                                                                                             opment aid issue.
                                                                                                                                                                                                Sweden was unable to cope
                                                                                                                                                                                             during the 19th century and a
                                                                                                                                                                                             third of its population fled to the
                                                                                                                                                                                             US to escape poverty. Post-war
                                                                                                                                                                                             Europe was unable to manage
                                                                                                                                                                                             until Marshall Aid helped get the

                                                                                                                                                                                             welfare-promoting forces of glo-
                                                                                                                                                                                             balisation - or dread the effects
                                                                                                                                                                                             it has on poor countries and
                                                                                                                                                                                             groups. The truth is that the
                                                                                                                                                                                             poorest countries of all are mar-
                                                                                                                                                                                             ginalised in relation to this glo-
                                                                                                                                                                                             balisation.
