                                                                                                                      Tender No. KJDI17/2000-2001 - Supply/Delivery of Road Safety materials
                                                                                                                      Tender No. KJDI18/2000-2001 - Paved/Unpaved roads routine maintenance works
                                                                                                                      Tenders are invited for the above supply/delivery of road safety materials and roads routine
                                                                                                                      maintenance works within Kajiado District for the period ending 30th June, 2001.
                                                                                                                      Interested tenderers must:
                                                                                                                      (i)     Be registered firms (attach registration certificate).
                                                                                                                      (ii)    Be registered with VAT (attach the certificate).
                                                                                                                      (iii)   Have PIN (Personal Identification Number).
                                                                                                                      In addition, tenderers interested in Tender No. KJD/18/2000-2001 (for paved and unpaved roads
                                                                                                                      routine maintenance works) must be registered with the Ministry of Roads and Public Works in
                                                                                                                      category E and below for resealing works.
                                                                                                                      Tender documents with detailed specifications may be obtained from the office of the District
                                                                                                                      Supplies Officer during the working hours upon payment of a non-refundable fee of Ksh 500 for
                                                                                                                      one set of documents per each tender.
                                                                                                                      Completed tender documents in plain sealed envelopes bearing no indication of the sender and
                                                                                                                      marked Tender No       for      should be addressed to:
                                                                                                                                                              District Commissioner
                                                                                                                                                                      P.O. Box 1
                                                                                                                                                                      Kajiado OR
                                                                                                                      Placed into the tender box situated at the main entrance to the District Commissioner's office so
                                                                                                                      as to reach him on but not later than 11th April, 2001 at 12.00 noon.
                                                                                                                      The Government reserves the right to reject or accept any tender and does not bind itself to give
                                                                                                                      reasons for it's decisions.
