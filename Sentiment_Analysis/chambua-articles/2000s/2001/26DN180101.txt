                The project has three sites in Kenya - Kajiado, Taita-Taveta and Turkana districts. In Turkana District
                the focus in interest are the Loima Mountains, seeking to develop community-based conservation
                initiatives.
                However the past drought and subsequent famine has delayed project implementation and so the
                project plans to increase interaction activities by recruiting a Deputy Field Project Officer to be based in
                Lodwar, Turkana District.
                Duties and Responsibilities
               The Deputy Project Officer will be responsible to the National Project Manager through day-to-day
               supervision of the Field Project Officer. It is envisaged that the Deputy Field Project Officer will
               strengthen site-based project reporting and workplan procedures. Specifically his/her responsibilities
               will be:
               1.     Assisting with the preparation, implementation and monitoring of site plans and activities.
               2.     Supporting partnership processes for project implementation.
               3.     Assisting with the facilitation of management programs for natural resources with local
                      communities.
               4.     Assisting with the promotion of conservation issues among local communities, CBOs, NGOs,
                      local authorities and Government agencies.
