INDIA HIV CASES BELOW UN ESTIMATES

New DELHI, Thursday

India has fewer than 2.5 millioon people living with HIV/AIDS,
a senior health official said yesterday, nearly 60 percent lower than the 5.7 million estimated by
the UN.
"The new number is just under 2.5 million," the official , who is closely involoved in Indias AIDS control programme, told Reuters on condition of anonymity, ahead of the official release of the numbers due on Friday, Tip officials met in New Delhi to finalise the new figure. (Reuters)
