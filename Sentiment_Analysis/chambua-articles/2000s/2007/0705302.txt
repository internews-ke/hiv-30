DAILY NATION Wednesday May 30, 2007

BRIEFLY

Busia

Worry as teachers
hit hard by HIV

The high number ofteachers
dying of HIV-related illnesses
is worrying the education fraternity
in the district. Some
47 teachers have died in the
district in the past one year,
the area education officer, Mr Musilu Kilonzo said.
The disease toll had heavily contributed to the shortage of teachers in the area,
Mr. Kilonzo said. He urged teachers to change their sexual behaviour to prevent the spread of HIV and AIDS.
